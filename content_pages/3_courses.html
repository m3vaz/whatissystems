Course Breakdown

The purpose of an enginereing undergraduate degree (Bachelor of Applied Sciences, if you're in Canada) is to provide intensive exposure to engineering concepts, with an emphasis on practical application. An engineering program will include courseware covering a wide range of concepts related to your specific discipline of study.

But you consider Systems Design Engineering as the study of how various engineering disciplines interact with each other, well, hopefully these re-arrangements and course breakdowns of our academic calendar cna help you understand what this all means.
